Privacy Policy

Entrevoz — Between Voices. Live. | Last updated: March 11, 2026

Summary: Entrevoz processes your voice for real-time translation and AI coaching. We do not store your conversations. We use Bluetooth only to route audio to your devices. We never sell your data.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Voice Data

When you use Entrevoz's translation or Wingman features, your voice is processed in real-time to convert speech to text. This processing happens through secure speech recognition and translation services. We do not store recordings of your voice or transcripts of your conversations.

1.2 Wingman Mode Data

When using Wingman Mode, Entrevoz listens to your conversations to generate real-time response suggestions. This data is:

  • Processed in real-time only
  • Used solely to generate suggestions
  • Not stored after the session ends
  • Never used for advertising or sold to third parties

1.3 Bluetooth & Audio Routing

Entrevoz uses Bluetooth to:

  • Detect connected audio devices (AirPods, Meta glasses, Bluetooth earphones)
  • Route AI coaching whispers to your preferred device
  • Discover nearby Entrevoz users for Face-to-Face translation

We broadcast only a random session identifier that changes each session. We do not broadcast your name, location, or any personal information.

1.4 Camera & Video Data

When using Video Call Mode, your camera captures video for live video calls. Video is streamed peer-to-peer using WebRTC encryption and is not recorded or stored by Entrevoz servers.

1.5 Device Information

We may collect anonymous device information for app analytics:

  • Device type and operating system version
  • App version
  • Crash reports (if enabled)

1.6 Account Information

Entrevoz core features work without an account. Optional account creation stores only your email for session history and settings sync.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Translation: Voice data is processed solely to provide real-time translation
  • Wingman: Conversation context is used only to generate response suggestions in real-time
  • Audio Routing: Bluetooth data routes AI whispers to your connected devices
  • Improvement: Anonymous analytics help us improve app performance

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties except:

  • Translation Services: Text (not audio) is sent to translation APIs to provide translations
  • AI Services: Conversation context is sent to AI services to generate Wingman suggestions
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law or legal process

4. Data Storage

Conversation data is ephemeral. When you end a conversation or Wingman session, the data is deleted. We do not maintain logs of your conversations.

Any data stored locally on your device (such as language preferences) remains on your device and is not transmitted to our servers.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Use Entrevoz without creating an account
  • Disable Bluetooth permissions (this will disable audio routing features)
  • Disable microphone permissions (this will disable voice features)
  • Disable camera permissions (this will disable video calls)
  • Delete the app and all local data at any time
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data

6. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:

  • All network communications use HTTPS/TLS encryption
  • Video calls use WebRTC end-to-end encryption
  • Bluetooth discovery uses randomized session identifiers
  • No persistent storage of conversation content

7. Children's Privacy

Entrevoz is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

8. International Data Transfers

Your data may be processed in the United States or other countries where our service providers operate. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for international transfers.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

10. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:
