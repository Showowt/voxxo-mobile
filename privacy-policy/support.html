How Entrevoz Works

Face-to-Face Mode

One phone, two people, two languages. Each person speaks — the other sees live translation on screen. No passing the phone. Just natural conversation.

Link Mode

Send a link. They tap it — no download needed. You speak, your words appear on their screen. They speak, their words appear on yours. Works anywhere.

Wingman Mode

AI coaching during calls. Entrevoz listens and generates three response options (Bold, Warm, Safe). The best one whispers through your AirPods or Meta glasses.

VoxNote

Translate voice messages before sending. Record in your language, review the translation, send with confidence.

Video Call Mode

Live-translated video calls with real-time subtitles. WebRTC powered, works anywhere in the world.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do people I'm talking to need to download the app?

No. For Link Mode, you send a web link — they tap it and the conversation happens in their browser. No download, no account needed.

How does Wingman Mode know what to suggest?

Wingman listens to your conversation in real-time and generates contextual response suggestions using AI. It understands the flow of conversation and suggests appropriate responses for dates, interviews, negotiations, or difficult talks.

Can people tell I'm using Wingman?

No. Suggestions are whispered through your AirPods or Meta glasses. Others see someone confident — they have no idea AI is coaching in real-time.

What devices work with Wingman?

AirPods (all models), Meta Ray-Ban Smart Glasses, Bose Frames, any Bluetooth earpiece/headphones, wired earphones, or phone speaker.

What languages does Entrevoz support?

30+ languages including Spanish, English, Portuguese, French, German, Italian, Japanese, Korean, Arabic, Mandarin, and more.

Is my conversation private?

Yes. Conversations are processed in real-time and are not stored. When you end a session, the data is deleted. See our Privacy Policy for details.

Does Entrevoz need internet to work?

Yes, real-time translation and Wingman AI require an internet connection.

Why isn't the microphone working?

Go to your device Settings > Entrevoz > Microphone and make sure permission is enabled. If already enabled, try restarting the app.

How do I connect my AirPods?

Make sure your AirPods are connected to your phone via Bluetooth. Entrevoz will automatically detect them and route Wingman whispers to your AirPods.

What are Wingman modes?

Date Mode: Confident, flirty, connection-focused suggestions. Interview Mode: Professional, memorable responses using STAR method. Sales Mode: Objection handling, value-focused responses. Hard Talk Mode: De-escalation, empathy, direct honesty.

How do I report a bug?

Email us at support@entrevoz.co with a description of the issue, your device model, and iOS/Android version.
