## Description (4,000 characters)

**Voxxo — Your Voice. Any Language. Instantly.**

Voxxo is the AI-powered real-time voice translation app built for real conversations — not tourist phrasebooks. Whether you're on a FaceTime call with someone who speaks Spanish, translating a WhatsApp voice note, or sitting across from someone at a bar in Medellín, Voxxo makes language invisible.

**FIVE MODES FOR EVERY SITUATION**

▁ **TYPE MODE**
Type in your language. Get an instant, verified translation. Copy it, share it, or send it directly to WhatsApp, iMessage, or any app. Verification shows you the back-translation so you know exactly what you're saying.

▁ **VOICE MODE**
Tap to record, or play a WhatsApp voice message on speaker — Voxxo transcribes and translates it instantly. Finally understand what your partner's mom is saying in those 3-minute voice notes.

▁ **FACE-TO-FACE MODE**
Place your phone between two people. Each speaks their own language. Voxxo translates both directions in real time on one device. Like having a personal interpreter that fits in your pocket.

▁ **VIDEO CALL MODE**
Video calls with live AI-translated subtitles. Share a room link — no account needed for the other person. WebRTC for crystal-clear, low-latency calls with TURN server fallback.

▁ **PROXIMITY CONNECT**
Discover nearby Voxxo users via Bluetooth. Connect with one tap. Like AirDrop for translated conversations. Perfect for bars, airports, conferences, and anywhere you meet someone new.

**12 LANGUAGES**

English • Spanish • French • Portuguese • German • Italian • Chinese • Japanese • Korean • Arabic • Russian • Hindi. More coming.

**WHY VOXXO**

• Four-tier AI translation engine — your message always gets through
• Privacy-first: peer-to-peer calls, nothing stored on servers
• No account needed for Face mode or receiving video calls
• Built by MachineMind in Colombia — we live the problem daily

Free to use. Voxxo Pro coming soon with unlimited translations and couples accounts.

## App Review Notes

```
No sign-in required for core functionality. The app uses Bluetooth LE for Proximity Connect discovery (requires user permission). Video Call mode uses WebRTC peer-to-peer connections with TURN server fallback. Translation is powered by a four-tier AI fallback system (local dictionary → MyMemory API → HuggingFace Helsinki-NLP models → passthrough). Speech recognition uses platform-native APIs on iOS. Face-to-Face mode works on a single shared device — no second account needed. All conversations are ephemeral and peer-to-peer; no user data is stored on our servers.
```
